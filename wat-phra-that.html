About Lampang

Lampang is a beautiful province in Northern Thailand, known for its rich history, charming temples, and scenic landscapes. Whether you're here for culture, nature, or relaxation, Lampang has something for everyone.

Lampang’s highlights include ancient temples, tranquil rivers, and unique local attractions. Explore the stunning views and immerse yourself in the warm hospitality of the locals.

Popular Attractions

Temple of the Emerald Buddha

Visit this ancient and sacred temple, one of the most revered in the country.

Lampang Elephant Sanctuary

Get up close with gentle giants at the Elephant Sanctuary and learn about their conservation.
